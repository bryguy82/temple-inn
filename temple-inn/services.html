Why Choose Us?

We provide the perfect environment for your family's special events. We have a highly adaptable and heavenly reception area you use for your wedding, missionary farewells, or any special occasion.

If you family history needs some work, we have plenty of resources to help you get the research done.

Some of the specialized services and features include:

  • A customizable wedding reception hall complete with simple catering and kitchen access
  • Free Access to locker rooms and bathrooms to change for temple patrons
  • A play area and a low-cost, short-term babysitting services for temple patrons' children
  • Long term accommodations with kitchenettes for full-time missionaries
  • A temple history themed reception and sitting area
  • A business center that supports family history and ordinance work

Some of the free amenities include:

  • Free continental breakfast
  • Exercise/workout room
  • Indoor pool
  • WiFi high-speed internet access and smart tvs
  • Parking
  • Family history consultation and family ordinance card services
